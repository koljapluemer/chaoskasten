Chaoskasten: Plaintext notes for productive people.

Idea

There are many ideas, concepts and tools to take notes.
Few work.

We aim to provide you:

  • A lean tool: No bloat, no drowning in features.
  • A tool to think with: In Chaoskasten, your knowledge is interconnected, just like your thoughts.
  • A learning tool: Every note is a flash card for spaced repetition learning.

Pricing

At any time, you can move to a permanent account for just $3/month.
